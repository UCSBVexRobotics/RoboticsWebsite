

This year, UCSB Robotics will mainly be focusing on a year-long project that involves a variety of different engineering fields: a waterproof robot to maneuver through the UCSB lagoon.

competitions/lagoonbot/lagoon.jpg

Anticipated core features:

- Submerging and surfacing easily from water

- Maneuvering through through the lagoon wirelessly

- Utilizing a camera

- Able to use a built in fail-safe mechanism to safely retrieve robot if power unexpectedly goes out

- Displaying real-time battery level

- Transmits a live video feed from the camera
